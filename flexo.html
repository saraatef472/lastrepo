In-Mold Labels For Blow Molding

Designed specifically for labeling blow-molded bottles, IML-B involves fusing the label to the container as it is being inflated. This fusing ensures the labels remain safely visible even when the contents are spilled or emptied. The label will never separate from the container, even when exposed to extreme environments. IML-B is suitable for PP bottles.

Wet Strength & Wet Glue:

We produce wet-glue labels for a wide range of applications. This is the classic label which is still very common. It is typically used for glass bottles, jars and plastic (PET, HDPE) containers. Wet-Glue Labels are labels that are not coated until the time of application. Printed paper or film substrates are coated with wet glue on the labelling line immediately before application. These labels can be produced also on special substrates like metallised papers. Wet-Glue labels can be laminated, cold-foil stamping and cut or die-cut to any shape.
